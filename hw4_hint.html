It's not hard to see that there are a ton of continuity problems with the graph.

Parameterize some curves in the xyxyxy-plane, compose them with fff, and plot the corresponding parametric curve in the graph of fff.

An easy way (read: free of headaches) to compose fff with parameterized curves is via the .subs() method.

var('t')
gamma1(t) = (t,t,f.subs({x:t},{y:t})) #curve on the graph of f lying above the line y=x (param as (t,t))
gamma2(t) = (t,1,f.subs({x:t},{y:1})) #curve on the graph of f lying above the line y=1 (param as (t,1))
plot4=parametric_plot3d(gamma1,(t,-5,-0.2),color='red',thickness=3)
plot5=parametric_plot3d(gamma1,(t,0.2,5),color='red', thickness=3)
plot6=parametric_plot3d(gamma2,(t,-5,-0.1),color='gold',thickness=3)
plot7=parametric_plot3d(gamma2,(t,0.1,5),color='gold', thickness=3)
show(plot1+plot2+plot3+plot4+plot5+plot6+plot7)
